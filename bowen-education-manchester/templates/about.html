About Bowen Education Group 关于博文集团

Bridging East and West Through Education Since 2018

自2018年起，中西融汇，博学致远

Our Mission

我们的使命

To provide exceptional Chinese language education that bridges cultural understanding and academic excellence. We empower students to succeed in a globalized world while maintaining deep connections to Chinese culture and heritage.

提供卓越的中文教育，架起文化理解与学术卓越的桥梁。我们赋予学生在全球化世界中取得成功的能力， 同时保持与中华文化和传统的深厚联系。

Our Vision

我们的愿景

To be the leading Chinese education institution in the UK, recognized for our innovative teaching methods, strong community partnerships, and commitment to nurturing bilingual, bicultural global citizens.

成为英国领先的中文教育机构，以创新的教学方法、强大的社区合作伙伴关系， 以及培养双语、双文化全球公民的承诺而闻名。

Our Story

我们的故事

From a small language school to a comprehensive education group

2018

Foundation / 创立

Bowen Chinese School was founded in Manchester with a vision to provide quality Chinese language education to the local community. Started with just 30 students and 2 teachers.

博文中文学校在曼彻斯特成立，旨在为当地社区提供优质的中文教育。 起初只有30名学生和2名教师。

2019

Expansion / 扩张

Launched our Tuition Centre offering GCSE and A-Level Chinese preparation. Partnership established with Henan University for teacher training and cultural exchange.

推出辅导中心，提供GCSE和A-Level中文备考课程。 与河南大学建立合作伙伴关系，进行师资培训和文化交流。

2020

Community Programmes / 社区项目

Introduced Chess Club and Badminton Club to foster holistic development. Began organizing cultural events and celebrations.

引入象棋俱乐部和羽毛球俱乐部，促进全面发展。 开始组织文化活动和庆祝活动。

2021-2025

Growth & Recognition / 成长与认可

Grew to over 500 students with 100% GCSE pass rate. Became recognized as a leading Chinese education provider in Greater Manchester. Expanded to comprehensive education group offering diverse programmes.

发展至超过500名学生，GCSE通过率达100%。成为大曼彻斯特地区公认的领先中文教育提供者。 扩展为提供多样化课程的综合教育集团。

Our Partnership

合作伙伴

Henan University, China

中国河南大学

We are proud to partner with Henan University, one of China's prestigious institutions with over 100 years of history. This collaboration enables:

  • Teacher training and professional development programmes
  • Cultural exchange opportunities for students
  • Access to authentic teaching materials and resources
  • Summer study programmes in China
  • HSK examination preparation support

我们自豪地与拥有百年历史的中国著名学府河南大学合作。这项合作带来了： 师资培训和专业发展项目、学生文化交流机会、正宗教材和资源、中国暑期学习项目、HSK考试备考支持。
